Me and my wife bought a curtain rod. It came with this nice template to cutout and place the brackets. We ended up losing the paper and our next set of brackets didn't come with the template.

So I looked online and couldn't find any templates to print out. So I made my own and wanted to share that with you and let you know how to use it.

Step 1: Print Out

Make sure to set the settings to print "actual size"

Step 2: Cut it out

You can cut the line to the short bracket or the long bracket depending on what you are hanging

Step 3: Measure the Screw Holes

Place the inside of the bracket next to the 3in mark.

Use a pencil/pen to measure where you want to put the screws.

Step 4: Place template on window corner

Start with the right side and place the template in the corner of the window.

Step 5: Drill in screws

Use your hole markings to drill into the wall. You don't need to drill all the way, just enought to mark the wall. You could also push against the screw to mark the holes.

Step 6: Flip paper and go to left side

On the left side you can now use the same holes on the paper.

Step 7: Put up curtain rod brackets

Now you have all 4 markings at an equal distance on both side. This make it super easy to get a good looking rod
